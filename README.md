Objective:

1)This Tableau project analyzes real estate sales data from 1998 to 2022 to understand property distribution, sales trends, and regional performance. 

2)The objective is to identify which property types contribute most to total sales and how sales vary across states and over time.

Key Findings:-

1)Single Family homes dominate the market, contributing the largest share of sales,

2)Followed by condos and apartments. States like Arkansas and North Carolina lead in total sales, indicating strong regional demand. 

3)The yearly trend reveals steady growth with a significant peak around 2021, followed by a slight decline in 2022, suggesting possible market correction.

Conclusion:

The project highlights the dominance of residential properties and regional disparities in sales. 
These insights can help stakeholders make informed decisions regarding investment, pricing strategies, and market focus.
